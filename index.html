A market terminal for Hypixel Skyblock

Ledger tracks bazaar prices, finds profitable order and craft flips, and scans the auction house for underpriced listings using true item attributes instead of display names. No account, no install — it runs in your browser.

Open the market terminal Look up an item

Bazaar & order flips

Every bazaar product with live buy/sell prices and volume, plus a ranked list of profitable order flips net of the bazaar fee.

Craft & book flips

Recipes priced against live ingredient costs, and enchanted-book combine chains detected straight from the bazaar's own product list.

Auction snipes

Decodes each listing's item data client-side and groups by real attributes — stars, recombobulation, enchants — so underpriced items don't hide behind a shared display name.

Item auction tracker

Look up any item to see live listings grouped by attribute variant, recent sales, a price history graph, and an in-game-style preview for any listing.

NPC & market trend flips

Reverse NPC flips, plus 24 hour top movers and low-supply markets once the optional backend has been running a while.

Runs entirely client-side

No account, no tracking, no server required for the core app. The optional backend is something you run yourself if you want sale history.

What this is, and isn't

Ledger is a research tool. Every figure is an estimate based on current listings, not a guarantee your orders fill at the prices shown. It's an independent, non-commercial project built for the Skyblock trading community, and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Hypixel Inc. or Mojang.
